About the Lab

The Intelligent Data Science Laboratory (IDSLab) at Seoul National University's Graduate School of Business focuses on cutting-edge research in data science, machine learning, and their applications to business and societal challenges. Our interdisciplinary approach combines rigorous theoretical foundations with practical applications, advancing the field through innovation and collaboration.

Big Data Analytics

Advanced techniques for processing and analyzing large-scale datasets

Machine Learning

Deep learning, predictive modeling, and intelligent algorithms

Agent-Based Modeling

Simulation and analysis of complex adaptive systems
